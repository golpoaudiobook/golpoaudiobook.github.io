Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27.1.2024

Welcome to Golpo: Discover the joy of story. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you use Golpo, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To personalize your experience.
  • Email Address: For account registration, password recovery, and communication purposes.

b. Usage Data

We collect data about how you interact with our app, such as the audiobooks you listen to, app features you use, and your preferences. This helps us improve our services and tailor them to your interests.



2. Use of Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To manage your registration as a user of Golpo.
  • Communication: To contact you with updates, security alerts, and support-related issues.
  • Service Improvement: To enhance the functionality and user experience of our app.



3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be disclosed to:

  • Service Providers: To facilitate our service; to provide the service on our behalf; to perform service-related services; or to assist us in analyzing how our service is used.
  • Legal Requirements: In response to lawful requests by public authorities or for legal, safety, or security reasons.


4. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.


5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Information, please contact us.


6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We advise you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.



7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@golpobd.com.
